About Us

Noah Valve Actuation, Inc. is a branch company of EMICO (Eunha Machinery Industrial Co.) in South Korea. EMICO, founded in 1986 is the leading manufactuer of electric actuators in Korea. We provide the best quality products with high technology, high performance and satisfactory services.

We supply electric actuators to buildings, plants, steelworks, powerplants, and more. Noah Actuators are supplied to over 30 countries. We are devoted to manufacturing high quality actuators at lower economical price to benefit our customers. We are focused on ensuring the best service to our distributors and customers in North America.

Noah Valve Actuation, Inc. is the sole supplier of all Noah actuators in the United States and have distributors around the country. Our manufacturing factory is located in South Korea but our main sales office is located in the economic center of the United States in Dallas, Texas.
